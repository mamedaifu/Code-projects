Empower

We empower and encourage female students to reach their full potential.

Inspire

Opportunities

We are here to provide a supportive environment, rich with opportunity to promote an interest in technology.


What We Stand For

Our key focus is overcoming the barriers that face women in the IT industry by removing the stigma associated with the typically male-dominated industry. We aim to promote a more inclusive industry, and as such, we aspire to empower women to be architects of change in the tech industry.

As the first society at UNSW catering specifically to female students in technology, we aim to connect members to industry sponsors through a variety of relevant events and activities, and to ensure that these students are equipped with technical skills and knowledge to ensure they are ready to face the workplace at the completion of their university experience.
